Common Issues

  • If purchases do not appear, check your network connection and try Restore Purchases.
  • If location features do not work, check iOS Settings and confirm location access is enabled for Trevio.
  • If trip discovery or routes fail, try again later; provider APIs may be temporarily unavailable.
