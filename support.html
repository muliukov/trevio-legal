What to Include in a Bug Report

Please include:

  • App version.
  • iPhone or iPad model.
  • iOS version.
  • City or destination.
  • Trip length / number of days.
  • Transport mode.
  • What you expected.
  • What happened instead.
  • Screenshot or screen recording if available.

Purchases and Subscriptions

If Trevio Plus is not active after purchase:

  1. Open Trevio.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Restore Purchases.

Subscriptions and refunds are managed by Apple through your Apple ID.

You can manage subscriptions in iOS Settings: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

Saved Trips

Free users can save 3 trips.

Existing saved trips remain available even without Trevio Plus.

Deleting a saved trip does not restore a free saved trip credit.
