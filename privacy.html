Trevio

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Overview

Trevio is designed to help you plan and save travel itineraries. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information You Provide

The app may store trip details you create, including destinations, selected places, custom stops, notes, visited or skipped places, passport memories, and saved trip photos.

Location

If you choose to use current location features, Trevio requests location access to suggest or center nearby destinations. Location access is optional and can be changed in iOS Settings.

Third-Party Services

Trevio may use third-party services such as Google Places, Google Routes, weather providers, public holiday data, and event context providers to generate travel suggestions. Requests may include destination names, coordinates, or route-related information needed to provide those features.

Purchases

Purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit. Trevio receives entitlement information, such as whether a subscription or lifetime unlock is active, but does not receive your full payment details.

Local Storage

Saved trips and passport data are stored on your device. Trevio may use the Keychain to store the lifetime free-save counter so it is not reset by reinstalling the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support from the Support page.
